## What We Believe

1. **Direction is scarcer than capacity.**
   In the arms race of infinite context, alignment is the narrow path that everyone overlooked.

2. **A dead end remembered is wisdom.**
   CDA does not promise you will find the right path on the first try. It promises you will not trip over the same dead end twice.

3. **Phase is identity.**
   `assemble` needs task state. `afterTurn` needs evaluation. `compact` needs topology. Treating every turn the same is not efficiency — it is erosion.

4. **Preserve the map, not the footprints.**
   SCG keeps the graph of reasoning intact. The tokens can go. The topology must survive.
